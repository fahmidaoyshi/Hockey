Q1: What is hockey?
Hockey is a team sport where two teams use sticks to hit a ball into the opponent’s goal. It’s a game of speed, skill, and teamwork.
Q2: How many players are there in a hockey team?
A hockey team has 11 players in total — 10 field players and 1 goalkeeper.
Q3: What is the main rule of hockey?
Players can only use one side of the stick to control the ball, and the main objective is to score goals in the opponent’s net.
